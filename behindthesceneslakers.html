#5. LeBron and LeBron....jr?

Yes, LeBron and his son Bronny James Jr. are teamates on the Los Angles Lakers. Bronny was selected by the Lakers as the 55th overall pick in the 2024 NBA Draft after one season at USC. Although Bronny is nowhere near as good as his father, his potential to grow and better himself is what keeps him on the team.
